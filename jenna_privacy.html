Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Chat with Jenna ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Device Identifier: We collect a unique device identifier (UUID) for authentication purposes. This identifier is generated locally on your device and does not contain personal information.
  • Chat Messages: Messages you send within the app are stored to provide conversation history and AI-powered responses.
  • Usage Data: We track basic usage metrics such as message counts and feature usage to improve the app experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate your device and maintain your session
  • Provide AI-powered chat responses
  • Store and display your conversation history
  • Enforce daily message limits
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support when needed

AI Processing

Your messages are processed using OpenAI's GPT technology to generate responses. Message content is sent to OpenAI's servers for processing but is not used to train their models. We do not share your personal information with OpenAI beyond what is necessary for generating responses.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers with industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

  • Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device
  • Server communications are encrypted using HTTPS
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes

Data Retention

We retain your chat history and account information for as long as you use the app. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at the email address below.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: For AI-powered chat responses
  • Mixpanel: For anonymous usage analytics

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
